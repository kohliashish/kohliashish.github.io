My Work Summary

Product leader with 7 years of experience in developing, and scaling technology products using data and AI/ML. Built products catering to over 80M customers with a combined impact of over $3B. Proven expertise in leading cross-functional teams, Data informed decision making, and leveraging AI/ML to build customer facing products.

My Work Experience

Senior Product Manager, Financial Analytics, Prime Video Finance

08/2023 – Current

  • Led the development of hyper-personalized recommendations on Prime Video by integrating data sources from channel partners and user signals from in-house teams (Alexa, Audible, Prime Music), resulting in a 13% improvement in viewing time and an increase in MAU by 15MM
  • Pioneered a 0 to 1 product for Prime Video store to surface vendor-sourced deals on TVOD and Channels, resulting in increased paid content engagement by 22% and an improvement in purchase conversion rate by 9%

Rolled out a successful pilot with a 26% improvement in ATF Click-through rate, followed by GA launch
  • Created a 1-year Product Roadmap for risk mitigation of royalty payments, leveraged qualitative data from Customer Interviews and launched Payment Tracker & Risk Manager product, increasing MoM Customer Retention by 6% and NPS by 40% across a $16B portfolio
Senior Product Manager Tech, Alexa Shopping

  • Launched an ML based Deal Recommendation Service, leveraging user data to suggest relevant and curated deals for customers across all Alexa surfaces, boosting Monthly Active Users by 1.2MM, and increasing attributed purchasers by 27% YoY

  • Created the Product Vision for AI-integrated customer experiences on Alexa leveraging in-house Large Language Models (LLMs), aligned Senior Leadership, and led the cross-team effort to pilot POC, resulting in 32% improvement in engagement (Goal Success Rate)
  • Revamped the Product Strategy for large scale sale events like Prime Day, Black Friday etc. by partnering with Data Science team to develop ML powered intent-based Customer segments and creating customized proactive prompts, leading to an incremental $1.5B annual revenue
  • Created a Product Roadmap and aligned Stakeholders to expand Alexa Proactive Deal experiences across multiple touchpoints like Smart home devices, Amazon app, and Fire devices increasing the MAU from 6MM to 18MM new users worldwide
  • Developed an Experimentation Framework to design, document and analyze A/B tests in a scalable manner and led multi-channel marketing acribution initiatives to expedite product launch decisions and generate an incremental revenue of $700M
Data Science Consultant, Mu Sigma Business Solutions
